FEAT-2001 to FEAT-2003 - Career Home Academy card routing and Academy card bar
Manual steps:
1. Open `/career/home` with at least one Academy card in the Card Library.
2. Verify the Academy card button reads `Go to Academy`.
3. Click `Go to Academy`.
4. On `/academy`, inspect the new Academy card bar above the player list.
Expected:
- Academy cards on Career Home route to `/academy`.
- Clicking `Go to Academy` does not apply or consume the card.
- The Academy page shows a horizontally scrolling Academy card bar with the player's Academy cards.

FEAT-2004 to FEAT-2006 - Maturity cards and Expand Academy handling
Manual steps:
1. Add one or more players to the academy.
2. Use `Organise Weak Friendly Game`, `Organise Average Friendly Game`, and `Organise Strong Friendly Game`.
3. Use `1 on 1 session` and `Train with the first team` and select individual academy players.
4. Use `Expand Academy` while below max capacity.
5. Use `Expand Academy` again when academy capacity is already `12`.
Expected:
- All-player maturity cards reduce all academy players by the exact defined amount.
- Single-target cards enter choose-target mode and only reduce the selected player by the exact amount.
- Maturity never drops below `0`.
- Players hitting `0` maturity gain normal mature-player handling and Academy alert behavior.
- `Expand Academy` adds one slot below max capacity.
- At capacity `12`, `Expand Academy` falls back to a `-3` all-player maturity effect.

FEAT-2007 to FEAT-2010 - Current/potential reveal flow and persistence
Manual steps:
1. Use `Run Training Drills`, `Full Training Games`, and `Mentor Sessions` on a populated academy.
2. Use `3v3 mini games`, `Classroom Lessons`, and `Video Analysis` on a populated academy.
3. Inspect academy players after each card use.
4. Promote a player whose current value and/or potential value has been revealed.
5. Open first-team management and inspect that promoted player.
Expected:
- Reveal cards use a two-step process:
  - best current staff `Judgement` filters affected players
  - card success chance then decides which affected players reveal the requested value
- `slight` = `25%`, `good` = `50%`, `excellent` = `75%`
- `Current Value` and `Potential Value` reveal independently and remain permanently visible once revealed.
- Revealed values remain visible in the Academy and stay represented after promotion.

FEAT-2011 to FEAT-2013 - Consumption rules, mature side effects, and debug support

- Navigation-only actions do not consume cards.
- Cancelling single-target mode does not consume the card.
