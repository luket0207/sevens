BUG-001 - Academy reveal cards should let the user choose which coach's Judgement is used
Status: fixed

Repro steps:
1. Open `/academy` with at least one reveal-style Academy card and multiple staff members.
2. Use a current-value or potential-value reveal card.
3. Check whether the card resolves immediately or prompts for coach selection.

Expected behavior:
- Reveal cards should open a coach-selection modal before rolling results.
- Only staff members not currently `inUse` should be selectable.
- The selected coach's `Judgement` should be the value used for the calculation.

Actual behavior:
- Reveal cards resolved without asking which coach should provide the Judgement value.

Suspected cause:
- Academy reveal logic auto-resolved to a best-available staff member centrally with no user selection step.

Attempted fixes:
- Added explicit Academy reveal-card detection and available-coach filtering.
- Reused the existing staff-selection modal flow for Judgement selection.
- Passed the selected coach into the centralized Academy card resolver.

Final fix:
- Reveal cards now open a modal to select which available, not-in-use coach provides the `Judgement` rating.
- The selected coach is passed into the Academy card resolver and recorded in the result/debug payload.
