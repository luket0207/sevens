EPIC-019 - Scouting Card Usage, Scouting Reports, and Academy Foundation

Overview
The goal of this epic is to implement how scouting cards actually work in the game.

This epic introduces:
- using scouting cards from the card library
- assigning a current staff member to go scouting
- locking that staff member as in use for the scouting duration
- marking scouting days on the calendar
- generating a scouting report when the scouting trip ends
- generating scouted players from scouting card rules
- partially revealing scouted player stats/traits using coach Judgement
- adding scouted players to a new Academy section of game state
- creating the Academy page
- academy slot rules
- academy maturity
- promoting matured players to the first team
- academy player loss to other clubs after maturity

This epic is about:
- scouting-card execution
- scouting trip lifecycle
- scouted player generation
- hidden/unhidden academy scouting intel
- academy storage and promotion flow

This epic is not about:
- changing the card reward system itself
- changing how players are fundamentally generated
- changing trait generation rules
- advanced scouting filters
- transfer systems
- money systems
- academy training cards or academy effects from other card types beyond storage foundations

================================================================
Core Scouting Card Rules
================================================================

Scouting cards need a new button:
- Scout

When the Scout button is clicked:
- show a modal
- let the user choose an available current staff member
- once chosen, that staff member becomes:
  - inUse = true
- while in use, that staff member cannot be used for anything else until returning from scouting

The scouting trip duration is determined by:
- the scouting card's Duration (Days) value

That duration should be counted from the current day.

Calendar marking rules:
- the scouting trip duration must be shown on the calendar
- mark the affected days with a small orange dot in the bottom right corner of the day tile
- when hovered, the tooltip shows the name of the staff member sent scouting
- if the duration is 1, only the next day is marked

When the final marked day is reached:
- before doing anything else that day
- before matches, draws, or normal continue flow
- the Continue button must switch into:
  - View Scouting Report
- this button should use:
  - Font Awesome icon faBinoculars

================================================================
Scouting Report Generation
================================================================

When the user clicks View Scouting Report:
- navigate to a new page called the Scout Report page

The scouting report page shows:
- the list of scouted players found during that session

How many players are generated:
- use the scouting card's Player Quantity per Session value
- use the selected coach's Scouting rating
- perform a figurative coin flip for each possible player slot
- chance of heads = coach Scouting rating
- chance of tails = 100 - coach Scouting rating
- number of heads = number of players generated for the report

This happens instantly.
No animation is required.

Example:
- Player Quantity per Session = 10
- coach Scouting rating = 62
- roll 10 times
- if heads occurs 6 times, then 6 players are generated

================================================================
Scouted Player Quality Resolution
================================================================

Each generated scouted player has a quality bucket determined by the scouting card's quality chances.

Scouting cards contain:
- Bad Player Chance
- Ok Player Chance
- Good Player Chance
- Great Player Chance
- Elite Player Chance

These values add up to 100 and act as weighted chances for each generated player.

Use these quality buckets to determine the player's overall and potential ranges:

Bad Player Chance
- Overall: 10-20
- Potential: 15-25

Ok Player Chance
- Overall: 15-25
- Potential: 25-35

Good Player Chance
- Overall: 15-30
- Potential: 30-40

Great Player Chance
- Overall: 20-35
- Potential: 35-45

Elite Player Chance
- Overall: 25-35
- Potential: 40-50

Rules:
- overall must fall within the chosen range
- potential must fall within the chosen range
- potential must be higher than overall

From then on:
- the player is generated like any normal player
- include:
  - normal position generation rules
  - influence rules
  - traits
  - name
  - appearance
  - etc.

Position chance:
- 10% chance the scouted player is a goalkeeper
- 90% chance the scouted player is outfield

================================================================
Hidden / Revealed Scouting Information
================================================================

On the scouting report page:
- there should be space to show all player values
- but by default all ratings are hidden
- traits are also hidden by default

Values that are never hidden:
- name
- appearance
- normal identity/display info
- player type context if useful

Values that always remain hidden at scouting stage:
- current overall
- potential

These do not participate in reveal checks and must always remain hidden on the scouting report and in the academy until promotion.

Hidden values that may be revealed through Judgement:

Goalkeepers
- Shot Stopping
- Distribution
- Control
- Traits

Outfield players
- Tackling
- Marking
- Passing
- Movement
- Control
- Dribbling
- Shooting
- Traits

Reveal process:
- use the chosen coach's Judgement rating / 2
- this becomes the reveal chance percentage for each individual rating and each individual trait
- check every rating independently
- check every trait independently

Example:
- coach Judgement = 40
- reveal chance = 20%
- every eligible rating gets its own 20% reveal roll
- every trait gets its own 20% reveal roll

Trait reveal notes:
- if a player has 2 traits, each trait is checked independently
- one may be visible while the other stays hidden

This reveal state must persist with the academy player data once added to the academy.

================================================================
Adding Players to the Academy
================================================================

At the bottom of each scouted player entry there should be a button:
- Add to Academy

When clicked:
- show an are you sure modal
- text:
  - Are you sure you want to add {playerName} to your academy?

If confirmed:
- add the player to a new game context section:
  - player academy

The academy stores:
- the player
- hidden/unhidden scouting reveal state
- maturity
- any other required scouting-origin metadata

Academy slot rules:
- academy slots are tied to staff slots:
  - 1 academy slot per staff slot
- academy slots can also be increased through other means later
- academy max slots = 12

This means:
- current academy capacity must be tracked
- academy add flow must respect capacity

Academy Page
================================================================

Add a new page:
- Academy

Add it to:
- the navigation on the home control panel

The Academy page should display:
- all players currently in the academy

Each academy player should show:
- normal player identity data
- the hidden/unhidden scouting state preserved from the scouting report
- maturity

For now:
- maturity should be displayed because it is needed for debugging in the next epic
- eventually it will become hidden later

================================================================
Maturity Rules
================================================================

When a player is added to the academy:
- generate a Maturity value
- random number between 60 and 200

Every day:
- maturity reduces by 1
- minimum is 0
- never goes negative

When maturity reaches 0:
- the player is considered mature
- show an ! icon on the Academy button on the home page
- show a new button on that player's academy entry:
  - Promote to First Team

Academy button notification rules:
- if the player opens the Academy page while there was an ! icon, remove the ! icon
- it stays gone until another academy event should make it appear again

================================================================
Promotion to First Team
================================================================

When the user clicks Promote to First Team:

If academy player is a goalkeeper:
- show a comparison modal
- compare the academy player's known values against the current goalkeeper
- allow:
  - Cancel
  - Replace current goalkeeper
- if replaced:
  - current goalkeeper is removed from the game
  - academy player joins first team
  - academy player becomes a normal player

If academy player is an outfield player:
- first show a modal where the user chooses which current outfield player to compare against
- then show the comparison modal
- if they cancel in the comparison modal:
  - return to the player-choice modal
- if they cancel from the player-choice modal:
  - close the whole promotion flow
  - nothing changes
- if they confirm replacement:
  - chosen current first-team outfield player is removed from the game
  - academy player joins first team
  - academy player becomes a normal player

Promotion reveal rule:
- when an academy player is promoted:
  - all hidden stats and traits become fully revealed
  - hidden/unhidden scouting data can be discarded
  - the player becomes a normal player from then on

================================================================
Mature Player Loss to Other Clubs
================================================================

Once a player is mature and remains in the academy, they may be lost to another club.

Protection window:
- for the first 14 days after maturity, they remain in the academy for certain

After that, each day there is a chance they are scouted by another club:

Days 15 to 50
- 0.5%

Days 51 to 112
- 1%

Days 113 to 162
- 2%

Days 163 to 224
- 5%

Days 225+
- 50%

If the player is scouted by another club:
- remove them from the academy immediately
- remove them from the game
- show ! on the Academy button

When the player next opens the Academy page:
- show a modal:
  - {playerName} was scouted by another club abroad.
- after acknowledging the modal:
  - continue to the Academy page
- then remove the ! icon again until another academy event occurs
