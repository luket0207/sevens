I want to make a new feature or this epic. I want to add kind of a judgement system to the scouting report. Right now players are rated as bad, ok, good, great or elite.

Coaches have a scouting value that will be between 1 and 100. 

I want it do that the coach gives their opinion on whether a player should be put in the academy or not.

The way I see it working is there will be 4 levels. 

Certain to be worth recruiting. 
Highly likely to be worth recruiting. 
Likely to be worth recruiting. 
No feedback

The coaches should take their best guess at these ratings for each player, but coaches with a lower rating should be more prone to not only missing players that would be worth recruting, but saying some more poor quality players would be worth recruiting. Coaches with higher scouting ratings should be far more reliable and get the judgement spot on. 

This will also be relative to the league as well. In league 5 scouting a great player is far more significant than if you were in league 1 and you scouted a great player. So in lower leagues the coaches should be more liberal with their rating.
