BUG-002

The card's layout looks really bad as the inconsistent heights are causing everything to be out of line. First of all, I want to give the cards a fixed height of 200px and the container should then handle that too. Then everything should be vertically aligned as flex-start to stop the weird resizing. 

Repro steps
1. Open `/career/card-reward` and inspect the offered cards row.
2. Open `/career/home` and inspect Card Library horizontal list.
3. Compare cards with short content vs long content text.

Expected behaviour
- Card tiles render at a fixed height of `200px`.
- Card list containers handle fixed card height consistently.
- Card rows align items with `flex-start` so cards do not stretch/misalign vertically.

Actual behaviour
- Card heights varied based on content, causing uneven rows and visual misalignment.
- Container alignment allowed inconsistent vertical card positioning/stretch behavior.

Suspected cause
- `careerCardTile` used content-driven height with no fixed height.
- Card row containers did not explicitly align items to `flex-start`.

Attempted fixes
- Updated base `careerCardTile` to fixed dimensions/behavior:
  - `height: 200px`
  - fixed width/flex basis for consistent row layout
  - column layout with action section anchored to bottom.
- Updated compact tile width/flex basis to remain consistent.
- Added `align-items: flex-start` and `min-height: 200px` to card scrollers in reward and library views.
- Added safe text clamping on card meta/effect text to prevent overflow-driven layout shifts.

Final fix
- Card tiles now render at fixed `200px` height.
- Reward/library horizontal containers align cards from `flex-start` and maintain stable vertical layout.
- Card rows no longer resize unevenly due to content length.

Files changed
- `src/game/cards/components/cardTile.scss`
- `src/game/cards/components/cardLibraryBar.scss`
- `src/game/cards/cardRewardChoice.scss`

Status: fixed
