EPIC-014 - Player Growth and Training System

Overview
This epic introduces the player growth and training system.

Training is the first system that allows players to improve their ratings over time.

Training works through:
- training sessions
- coaches
- success probabilities
- player potential limits
- sub-rating progression

Training sessions are scheduled on the calendar and executed automatically when their day arrives.

Training cannot be scheduled on match days.

Training success can improve player skill ratings using a sub-rating progression system.

This epic introduces:
- the training bar UI
- training session inventory
- drag/drop scheduling onto the calendar
- coach hiring and weekly availability
- automatic coach training session generation
- training session execution and player growth
- training results summaries

This epic does not introduce:
- advanced coach personalities
- coach progression
- player morale effects
- training injuries

Those systems may be introduced later.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Training System Core Rules
----------------------------------------------------------------

A training session can be scheduled on a calendar day.

Restrictions:
- training cannot be scheduled on match days
- training cannot be scheduled on the current day
- training cannot be scheduled on past days

Training sessions are placed by dragging them from the Training Bar onto valid calendar days.

Training sessions cannot be moved once scheduled.
They may only be:
- placed
- cancelled

A scheduled training session may be cancelled up until the day before it occurs.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Training Bar
----------------------------------------------------------------

A new Training Bar must appear directly under the calendar.

Requirements:
- same width as the calendar
- fixed height
- height remains constant even when empty

The Training Bar shows:
- all training sessions currently available to the user
- coach availability information

Training sessions behave like inventory items:
- sessions have quantities
- identical sessions stack
- quantity decreases when a session is scheduled
- if quantity reaches zero the session disappears

For debugging purposes:
- the player should start with 1 of every session

For the real game:
- the player starts with zero sessions

----------------------------------------------------------------
Coach System
----------------------------------------------------------------

Coaches can be hired from the Staff page.

For now provide:
- 10 coaches with different qualities

Coach properties:

Name
GK Rating (0-100)
DF Rating (0-100)
MD Rating (0-100)
AT Rating (0-100)
Tactics Rating (0-100)
Scouting Rating (0-100)
Player Judgement Rating (0-100)
Sessions per Week (1-3)
Wage (100-10,000)

Coach session availability resets weekly.

When a coach is used in training:
- one session is deducted from their weekly session pool

If no coaches are available:
- the training session is cancelled

FEAT-1406 - Weekly Coach Planning System
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Every Monday a coach may generate a training session for the player.

Chance calculation:

(GK + DF + MD + AT) / 4

Example:
0 + 20 + 60 + 20 = 100
100 / 4 = 25%

Therefore:
25% chance a session is generated.

Acceptance Criteria
- Monday modal appears
- coach generated sessions added to training bar

FEAT-1414 - Implement Sub-Rating Growth System
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Skill improvements accumulate as sub-rating points.

Rule:
To increase rating from N to N+1 requires N points.

Example:
rating = 21

needs:
21 points to reach 22.

Sub-points accumulate across sessions.

Acceptance Criteria
- ratings increase only when threshold reached
- excess points carry over
