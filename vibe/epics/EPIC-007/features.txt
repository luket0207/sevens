EPIC-007 - Career Calendar and Main Game Loop Structure

Overview
The goal of this epic is to build the main structural loop for the game around a seasonal calendar.

This epic establishes:
- the main /career/home structure
- the season calendar component
- the schedule/fixture generation for the season
- the main day-to-day progression flow
- the new flash modal component for day progression
- the first real framework for moving through time in Career Mode

Match generation itself is not in scope and must not be implemented or accounted for beyond scheduling fixtures into the calendar.

This epic is about:
- calendar structure
- schedule structure
- progression structure
- visualisation of events in time

It is not yet about:
- match simulation
- playing matches
- resolving cup outcomes
- resolving standings from played matches
- future-season transitions
- event consequences

================================================================
Core Structural Goals
================================================================

The first thing to do is remove the current debugging output on /career/home because it has served its purpose.

Then /career/home should become the main game loop screen for the career.

The layout should be:
- a calendar panel on the left taking roughly 70% of the width
- a control panel on the right for navigation/actions
- a debugging section at the bottom for future and current debug use

The calendar should show the current season schedule and allow the player to move through time.

Only one month should be visible at a time.

================================================================
Calendar Model
================================================================

A career can contain multiple seasons over time.

For this epic, we only need to build the structure for one season calendar and support the idea that future seasons can exist later.

One season calendar consists of:
- 4 months
- each month has 4 weeks
- each week has 7 days

This means one season is:
- 16 total weeks
- 112 total days

Only one month should be visible at once.
There should be basic pagination to move between the months.

The calendar should clearly show:
- what day is the current day
- what days contain matches
- what days contain cup draws

When showing a match in the calendar, display:
- the opponent
- the competition the match belongs to

================================================================
League Structure for Starting Career
================================================================

The player starts in League 5.

League 5 contains:
- the player team
- the 7 other generated League 5 teams

That gives:
- 8 teams total in League 5

A league season uses a double round-robin structure:
- every team plays every other team home and away

For an 8-team league this gives:
- 14 league matches per team

There are 16 weeks in the season, so league scheduling must account for:
- 14 league match weeks
- 2 non-league-match weeks

League schedule rules:
- league matches are played on Saturday or Sunday
- weeks 1 to 7 = league match weeks
- week 8 = break from league matches
- weeks 9 to 15 = league match weeks
- week 16 = reserved for league playoffs and cup finals

This means:
- weeks 1 to 7 each contain one league match
- week 8 contains no league match
- weeks 9 to 15 each contain one league match
- week 16 contains no normal league match

================================================================
Cup Competitions
================================================================

There are 2 cup competitions in the game.

----------------------------------------------------------------
League Cup
----------------------------------------------------------------

Participation:
- every team in Leagues 1 to 4 enters the League Cup

Competition format:
- random knockout cup
- 5 rounds total

Draw timing:
- the draw for the next round happens on the Thursday before the next round of matches is due

League Cup match weeks:
- week 2
- week 5
- week 8
- week 12
- week 16

League Cup match day rules:
- week 2 = Wednesday
- week 5 = Wednesday
- week 8 = Saturday
- week 12 = Wednesday
- week 16 = Wednesday

Special note:
- week 8 cup match is on Saturday because there are no league matches that weekend

----------------------------------------------------------------
Champions Cup
----------------------------------------------------------------

Participation:
- top 4 teams from League 1 from the previous season
- plus 12 foreign teams
- 16 total teams

Competition format:
- 4 groups of 4
- each team plays each other team in its group once
- top 2 from each group qualify for knockout stage

Knockout quarter-final structure:
- QF1 = Winner Group A vs Runner-up Group D
- QF2 = Winner Group B vs Runner-up Group C
- QF3 = Winner Group C vs Runner-up Group B
- QF4 = Winner Group D vs Runner-up Group A

Semi-finals:
- drawn at random from the quarter-final winners
- home team/random ordering should also be randomised

Final:
- drawn at random from the semi-final winners
- home team/random ordering should also be randomised

Champions Cup match weeks:
- week 1
- week 4
- week 8
- week 11
- week 14
- week 16

Champions Cup match day rules:
- weeks 1, 4, 8, 11, 14 = Wednesday
- week 16 = Saturday

================================================================
Playoff Match Rules
================================================================

A playoff match can also appear in the calendar.

Playoff eligibility:
- Leagues 5, 4, 3, and 2
- if a team finishes 2nd or 3rd in those leagues
- those 2 teams play each other in a playoff
- the winner gets promoted

Playoff timing:
- week 16
- Saturday

Important rule:
- it is impossible for a Champions Cup team to also be in a playoff
- so these week 16 Saturday events do not conflict

For this epic, playoff scheduling structure should exist in the calendar model, but no outcome resolution is required yet.

================================================================
Day Progression
================================================================

The player must be able to move through days in the calendar.

When moving to a new day:
- a modal-like visual must appear showing that a new day has started
- for this, do not use the existing modal component
- instead, create a new component called Flash Modal

Flash Modal rules:
- takes:
  - content
  - duration in seconds
- content is displayed in the centre of the screen
- dark overlay behind it
- user cannot close it manually
- user cannot interact with the app while it is active
- it auto closes after the duration
- then control returns to the app

The day progression feature must use this Flash Modal to show what day has started.

Important rule:
- it should only appear when a new day starts
- it should not repeat again during the same day
- it should only trigger once per day transition

For events on the calendar:
- when reaching them for now, do nothing
- they are placeholders for future logic

================================================================
Layout Requirements
================================================================

/career/home should use this general layout:

Main layout:
- left panel: calendar area, about 70% width
- right panel: control area
- bottom section: debugging area

Calendar panel:
- shows one month at a time
- supports month pagination
- shows current day and scheduled events

Control panel:
- continue / move to next day actions
- future navigation links to other pages
- simple structural controls for now

Debug section:
- reserved for later use
- can contain schedule/debug output as needed during implementation
