EPIC-018 - Staff Card Usage, Staff Slots, and Staff Progression

Overview
The goal of this epic is to implement how staff cards actually affect the game.

Until now, staff cards have existed mainly as collectible/library items.
This epic makes them usable.

This epic introduces:
- active hiring of Staff Member cards
- active use of Staff Upgrade cards
- staff slot limits
- staff slot progression tied to first-time promotion
- staff card expiry for unused Staff Member cards
- removal of used staff cards from the library
- replacement/sacking flow when staff capacity is full

This epic is about:
- staff card usage
- team staff management rules
- staff slot progression
- staff member replacement flow
- staff card expiry handling

This epic is not about:
- changing how staff cards are generated
- changing the staff card reward matrix
- changing what staff do in training/scouting resolution beyond being upgraded/hired
- salary/wage costs
- money systems
- staff personalities
- staff moving between teams

================================================================
Core Staff Rules
================================================================

The player begins the game with:
- 2 members of staff already hired
- 2 total staff slots

This means:
- the player starts with a full staff team
- they cannot hire a 3rd member of staff until they unlock another slot

The player can still obtain additional staff cards while already full:
- this is fine
- those cards go into the card library as normal
- they can later be hired if space becomes available, or by replacing an existing staff member

================================================================
Staff Slot Progression Rules
================================================================

Staff slots increase through promotion progression.

Rule:
- when the team is promoted to a league they have never reached before in that career, they gain 1 extra staff slot

Examples:
- start in League 5 with 2 staff slots
- first promotion to League 4 -> gain +1 slot
- first promotion to League 3 -> gain +1 slot
- first promotion to League 2 -> gain +1 slot
- first promotion to League 1 -> gain +1 slot

This means:
- if the player reaches League 1 for the first time in that career, they will have 6 staff slots total

Important permanence rules:
- gained slots are permanent for the rest of the career
- if the player is relegated, they do not lose the slot(s)
- if the player is promoted back to a league they have already reached before, they do not gain another slot
- the player only gains a new slot when reaching a higher league than they have ever reached before in that career

This means the career must track:
- highest league reached so far
- current total staff slots unlocked

================================================================
Staff Member Cards
================================================================

Staff Member cards should now be usable from the card library.

Each Staff Member card in the library should show:
- its existing staff information
- a button labelled:
  - Hire

When Hire is clicked:

Case 1 - space available
- if the player has an open staff slot
- the staff member is immediately added to the team staff
- the card is consumed
- the card is removed from the library

Case 2 - no space available
- if the player already has a full staff team
- the user must be given a choice to:
  - sack an existing staff member and replace them with the new one
  - or cancel
- if they choose to sack:
  - the chosen current staff member is permanently removed from the game
  - the new staff member is added to the team
  - the card is consumed
  - the card is removed from the library
- if they cancel:
  - nothing changes
  - the card remains in the library
  - no existing staff member is removed

Important rule:
- sacked staff members are removed from the game completely
- do not return them to the library
- do not keep them in a hidden pool

================================================================
Staff Member Card Expiry
================================================================

Unused Staff Member cards should expire.

Rule:
- 7 days after the card was collected, the card expires
- expired Staff Member cards are removed from the library
- the card is lost permanently

Requirements:
- the expiry value/date must be displayed on the Staff Member card
- expiry should be based on the in-game calendar/day system
- once expired, the card should no longer be usable

Scope note:
- this expiry rule applies to Staff Member cards in the library
- do not apply this rule to staff who are already hired
- do not apply this rule to Staff Upgrade cards unless explicitly added later

================================================================
Staff Upgrade Cards
================================================================

Staff Upgrade cards should now be usable from the card library.

Each Staff Upgrade card in the library should show:
- its existing upgrade information
- a button labelled:
  - Use

When Use is clicked:
- open a modal showing the list of current staff members
- the player chooses which current staff member to apply the upgrade to
- the effect of the upgrade card is applied to that chosen staff member

Upgrade rules:
- staff ratings are capped at 100
- a stat cannot be upgraded beyond 100
- if the upgrade would exceed 100, clamp it to 100
- the upgrade applies only to the targeted staff member chosen in the modal

After successful use:
- the upgrade card is consumed
- the upgrade card is removed from the library

If the user cancels out of the modal:
- no effect is applied
- the card remains in the library

================================================================
Card Consumption Rules
================================================================

A staff card should only be removed from the library when its effect has actually been used successfully.

Remove the card when:
- a Staff Member card is successfully hired
- a Staff Member card is successfully used to replace/swap with a currently employed staff member
- a Staff Upgrade card is successfully applied to a staff member

Do not remove the card when:
- the player opens Hire but cancels
- the player opens the replacement flow but cancels
- the player opens Use on a Staff Upgrade card but cancels before applying it
- the card has not yet been successfully resolved

================================================================
Integration Rules
================================================================

This epic should integrate with:
- the card library
- the in-game calendar/day progression
- the player's current staff state
- the staff slot state
- promotion progression state

The implementation should preserve the existing staff-card generation system and simply add the usage/application behaviour on top of it.

Feature List
================================================================

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1801 - Add Persistent Team Staff State with Starting Staff
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Create the active team staff state used by the career and populate it with the player's starting staff.

Requirements
- The player should begin the game with 2 hired staff members already active on the team.
- The player should begin with 2 total staff slots.
- The team staff state should be stored in career state and be available to the card library and future systems.
- Keep the structure easy to read and reusable for future staff-related features.

Acceptance Criteria
- A new career starts with 2 active staff members.
- A new career starts with 2 staff slots.
- Team staff state persists in career data.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1802 - Track Highest League Reached and Permanent Staff Slot Unlocks
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Track the player's highest-ever league reached so staff slot unlocks can be awarded only once per new tier reached.

Requirements
- Store the highest league reached in the current career.
- When the player is promoted to a league they have never reached before, unlock exactly 1 additional staff slot.
- Do not unlock another slot for reaching a league already reached previously.
- Do not remove unlocked slots after relegation.
- Continue this progression until the player has reached League 1, resulting in 6 total slots.

Acceptance Criteria
- Staff slots increase only on first-time promotion to a new highest league.
- Relegation does not remove unlocked slots.
- Re-promotion to a previously reached league does not give another slot.
- Reaching League 1 for the first time results in 6 total slots.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1803 - Display Available Staff Slots and Current Staff Count
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Expose the player's staff capacity clearly in the relevant UI.

Requirements
- Show current staff count versus maximum staff slots where appropriate in staff-related UI.
- This should support the user understanding whether they can hire immediately or need to replace someone.
- Keep the display simple and clear.

Acceptance Criteria
- The player can see how many staff they have and how many slots are available.
- It is clear when the staff team is full.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1804 - Add Hire Action to Staff Member Cards in the Library
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Make Staff Member cards in the card library actively hireable.

Requirements
- Staff Member cards should display a button labelled:
  - Hire
- Clicking Hire should attempt to add the staff member to the player's active staff.
- If there is an open staff slot, hire immediately.
- On successful hire:
  - add the staff member to active staff
  - remove the card from the library

Acceptance Criteria
- Staff Member cards show a Hire button.
- Clicking Hire adds the staff member immediately if space is available.
- Successfully hired staff cards are removed from the library.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1805 - Add Staff Replacement / Sacking Flow When Hiring at Full Capacity
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Handle Staff Member hiring when the player already has a full staff team.

Requirements
- If the player clicks Hire on a Staff Member card while all staff slots are full:
  - open a replacement/sacking flow
- Show the user's current staff list
- Allow the player to:
  - choose an existing staff member to sack and replace with the new one
  - or cancel
- If a current staff member is chosen:
  - remove that staff member from the game completely
  - add the new staff member to active staff
  - remove the hired card from the library
- If the user cancels:
  - no change happens
  - the card remains in the library

Acceptance Criteria
- Hiring at full capacity opens a replacement flow.
- The player can choose an existing staff member to sack.
- Sacked staff are removed from the game completely.
- The new staff member replaces them successfully.
- Cancelling leaves everything unchanged.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1806 - Add Staff Member Card Expiry System
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Make unused Staff Member cards expire after 7 in-game days.

Requirements
- Every Staff Member card placed into the library should store when it was collected.
- 7 days after collection, if the card is still unused in the library, it expires.
- Expired Staff Member cards should be removed from the library automatically.
- Expired cards are lost permanently.
- Do not apply this expiry to already hired staff.

Acceptance Criteria
- Staff Member cards track their collection timing.
- Unused Staff Member cards expire 7 in-game days later.
- Expired Staff Member cards are removed from the library.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1807 - Display Staff Member Card Expiry Information in the Library
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Show the expiry value/date for Staff Member cards in the card library.

Requirements
- Each Staff Member card in the library should display its expiry information clearly.
- The player should be able to understand how long they have left to use the card.
- Use the in-game day/calendar context rather than real-world time.

Acceptance Criteria
- Staff Member cards display expiry information.
- The user can clearly see that these cards will expire if unused.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1808 - Add Use Action to Staff Upgrade Cards in the Library
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Make Staff Upgrade cards actively usable from the library.

Requirements
- Staff Upgrade cards should display a button labelled:
  - Use
- Clicking Use should open a modal listing the player's current staff.
- The player can choose one staff member to receive the upgrade.
- If successfully applied:
  - update the chosen staff member
  - remove the Staff Upgrade card from the library
- If cancelled:
  - no effect is applied
  - the card remains in the library

Acceptance Criteria
- Staff Upgrade cards show a Use button.
- Clicking Use opens a staff-selection modal.
- Successfully used upgrade cards are removed from the library.
- Cancelling keeps the card in the library.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1809 - Apply Staff Upgrade Effects with Rating Cap of 100
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Apply Staff Upgrade card effects to the selected staff member while respecting stat caps.

Requirements
- Read the chosen Staff Upgrade card effect.
- Apply it to the chosen staff member's relevant rating.
- Staff member ratings cannot exceed 100.
- Clamp upgraded values at 100 if necessary.
- Keep the upgrade application logic centralised and reusable.

Acceptance Criteria
- Staff upgrades correctly modify the intended rating.
- Ratings never exceed 100.
- The applied changes persist on the selected staff member.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1810 - Remove Used Staff Cards from the Library Only on Successful Use
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Enforce correct card-consumption rules for staff cards.

Requirements
- Remove a Staff Member card from the library only when:
  - it is successfully hired
  - or successfully used to replace an existing staff member
- Remove a Staff Upgrade card from the library only when:
  - it is successfully applied to a staff member
- Do not remove a card when the player cancels out before applying its effect.

Acceptance Criteria
- Used staff cards are removed only after successful application.
- Cancelled actions do not consume the card.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1811 - Integrate Staff Card Usage with Calendar Day Progression
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Ensure staff card expiry and staff state update correctly as the in-game days advance.

Requirements
- Tie Staff Member expiry checks into the in-game calendar/day progression system.
- As days advance, expired Staff Member cards should be cleaned from the library.
- Ensure this system works consistently across normal progression.

Acceptance Criteria
- Staff card expiry updates correctly as the calendar advances.
- Expired cards disappear at the correct in-game time.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FEAT-1812 - Add Staff Card Usage Debug/Test Support
----------------------------------------------------------------

Description
Provide lightweight debugging support for validating staff card usage, expiry, and slot progression.

Requirements
- Add development-friendly visibility for:
  - current staff members
  - unlocked staff slots
  - highest league reached
  - pending expiring Staff Member cards
  - Hire/Use resolution outcomes
  - replacement flow behaviour
- Keep this lightweight and useful for implementation/testing.

Acceptance Criteria
- Developers can inspect the key state behind staff card usage.
- Slot unlocking, expiry, hiring, and upgrades are easier to validate.

================================================================
Epic Completion Criteria
================================================================

This epic is complete when:

- The player starts with 2 active staff members and 2 staff slots.
- Staff slots increase by 1 only when the player reaches a new highest league for the first time.
- Staff slots are permanent once unlocked.
- Staff Member cards show a Hire button.
- Staff Member cards can be hired if staff space is available.
- If no staff space is available, the player can sack an existing staff member or cancel.
- Sacked staff are removed from the game completely.
- Staff Member cards expire 7 in-game days after collection if unused.
- Staff Member cards display their expiry information in the library.
- Staff Upgrade cards show a Use button.
- Staff Upgrade cards can be applied to a chosen current staff member through a modal.
- Staff ratings are capped at 100 when upgraded.
- Staff cards are only removed from the library when successfully used.
- Cancelled staff card actions do not consume the card.
- Staff card usage is integrated with the in-game calendar and staff state.
