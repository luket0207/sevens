EPIC-018 Implementation Log
Date: 2026-03-12

Scope implemented:
- Added persistent active-staff state with slot capacity and highest-league progression tracking.
- Added first-time-promotion staff-slot unlock progression (permanent unlocks, no relegation loss).
- Added staff-card usage in Card Library:
  - Staff Member: `Hire` action with immediate hire when space exists.
  - Staff Member at full capacity: replacement/sacking modal flow.
  - Staff Upgrade: `Use` action with target-staff selection modal.
- Added Staff Member card expiry (7 in-game days) with library display and day-advance cleanup.
- Enforced card consumption only on successful staff-card resolution.
- Added staff usage/expiry/slot debug visibility in Career Home debug panel.
